About
My name is Zhaohan(Chester) Pan. I am currently a Master's student in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University, and also the Director and Principal Investigator of the CRAFT^2 Lab.

I am passionate about designing and developing computer interfaces while exploring how users perceive them to reimagine Human-Computer Interaction. My works focus on HCI topics like haptics, augmented reality, and sonification, with applications in gaming, accessibility, and more.
